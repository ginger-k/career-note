1.安装sublime

2.下载插件：https://github.com/tanepiper/SublimeText-Nodejs

3.解压到SublimeText3\Data\Packages，重命名为Nodejs

4.打开SublimeText3\Data\Packages\Nodejs\Nodejs.sublime-settings
	"node_command": false 改为 "node_command": "D:\\Program Files\\nodejs\\node.exe" 
	"npm_command": false 改为 "npm_command": "D:\\Program Files\\nodejs\\npm.cmd" 
	
5.打开SublimeText3\Data\Packages\Nodejs\Nodejs.sublime-build
	"encoding": "cp1252" 改为 "encoding": "utf8"
	"cmd": ["taskkill /F /IM node.exe & node", "$file"] 改为 "cmd": ["node", "$file"]
	
6.编写一个测试文件test.js，按"ctrl+B"运行代码
